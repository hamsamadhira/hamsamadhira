More about me

Builder. Performer.
Perpetually curious.

Hi, I'm Hamsa — a Computer Science & Neuroscience student who loves building things that feel both technically sharp and deeply human. I'm especially excited by projects where software does more than function — where it can adapt, reason thoughtfully, and support people in meaningful ways.

That's part of why I gravitate toward agentic AI systems, machine learning pipelines, and workflow automation — particularly in health-tech, where the stakes make the craft matter more.

Outside of code, I'm someone who feels deeply connected to music, travel, performance, and visual atmosphere. I notice color, sound, movement, architecture, and the small details that make a moment memorable. This site reflects all of those different sides — the builder, the performer, the observer, and the person constantly curious about both people and technology.
