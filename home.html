ABOUT ME

A digital corner for the spaces I come alive in.

Hi, I’m Hamsa. I’m a Computer Science and Neuroscience student who is deeply drawn to music, performance, creativity, travel, and meaningful experiences. I wanted this site to feel like more than a class assignment. I wanted it to feel like a real reflection of me — dark, bold, expressive, and full of motion. Some parts of me show up through music and performance, some through coding and building, some through the places I travel to, and some through the memories and visuals I collect along the way.

I love the intersection of structure and feeling. Computer science gives me a way to create, problem-solve, and build systems that make ideas real. Neuroscience gives me a way to think about people more deeply — memory, attention, emotion, and what shapes the way we experience the world. Beyond academics, I’m someone who notices atmosphere: color, sound, architecture, movement, and the small details that make a moment memorable.

This website is built around those different sides of me. It’s part portfolio, part visual diary, part personal archive. The sections below represent the spaces where I feel most like myself: where I perform, where I create, where I build, where I collect, where I wander, and where I think.
