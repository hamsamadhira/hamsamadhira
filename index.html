Welcome to My Website

Hello! My name is Hamsa, and this website is a small introduction to who I am and what I care about. I am someone who enjoys learning, building new ideas, and exploring creative ways to combine technology, leadership, and personal growth. I am especially interested in how websites, design, and digital tools can help people communicate their stories in a meaningful and engaging way. That is one reason I enjoyed building this site. It gave me the chance to not only practice HTML and CSS, but also to think about how design choices affect a visitor’s experience.

Outside of academics, I am passionate about projects that involve creativity, communication, and problem-solving. I enjoy working on ideas that bring structure to information while also making it visually appealing. Whether I am organizing content, writing, or designing something simple but polished, I like creating work that feels both useful and personal. I also value growth, curiosity, and consistency. These qualities motivate me to keep learning new technical skills and improve with each project I complete.

This website highlights a little about me and one of the areas I enjoy most: blending personal interests with digital creation. On the next page, I share more about some of the topics and activities I care about, along with a few examples that reflect my interests. My goal for this website is to create something clean, welcoming, and easy to navigate while meeting all the project requirements. I hope it gives a clear sense of who I am and what I enjoy. Thank you for visiting my website.
