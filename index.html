About Me

Where science, storytelling, and curiosity meet

I am a student who loves living at the intersection of logic and feeling. Computer science gives me a way to build, solve problems, and think in systems. Neuroscience gives me a way to understand people more deeply — how we think, remember, feel, and experience the world. Together, they shape the way I see both technology and human life.

Outside of academics, music and travel are two of the biggest parts of who I am. Music gives shape to my memories. Certain songs can instantly bring back a time, a place, or a version of myself. Travel does something similar. It makes life feel bigger. I love city streets, beautiful architecture, waterfronts, cafés, concerts, and moments that feel cinematic enough to remember forever.

This website is a collection of those interests. It’s a space where I can present myself not only as a student, but as a person with a creative point of view. I wanted the design to feel warm, modern, and colorful, with room for both personal reflection and visual energy. More than anything, I wanted it to feel authentic.
