NOW TUNING TO: me@stage

Hi, I’m Hamsa. I’m a Computer Science and Neuroscience student, but I’m also much more than a major. I love music performances, travel, visual storytelling, vibrant spaces, and ideas that sit between logic and feeling. I wanted this website to feel like a real digital corner of my life — not just a static introduction, but a place that reflects my different facets and the energy I bring into everything I do.

Some parts of me show up on stage, some in code, some in airport windows, some in art spaces, and some in the way I think about the brain and human behavior. This site is built around that idea: that identity is layered, and that the most interesting people are rarely just one thing. I’m curious, expressive, ambitious, and always drawn to experiences that feel meaningful and memorable.

The design of this homepage is meant to reflect that feeling. It is darker, sharper, more interactive, and more alive — inspired by digital studios and immersive portfolio spaces. On the next page, I break down those different sides of myself more clearly, from music and performances to code projects, travel, art, and the intersection of computer science and neuroscience.
