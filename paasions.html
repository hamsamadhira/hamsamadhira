FACETS

the different spaces that make me, me

me@stage

One of the strongest and most expressive parts of me shows up through music and performance. Being on stage is not just about singing or performing; it is about energy, connection, and presence. Music gives shape to emotion in a way that words sometimes cannot. A performance can create memory instantly, both for the audience and for the person on stage. That is one of the reasons this side of me feels so important. It is where confidence, emotion, rhythm, and personality all come together in a visible way.

me@code

Another major side of me lives in code. As a Computer Science student, I love the structure, challenge, and creativity involved in building technical projects. Coding gives me a way to take an idea and turn it into something real. Whether I’m solving a problem, designing a user experience, or organizing logic into a system, I enjoy the clarity and momentum that comes from creating something functional. This part of me reflects curiosity, discipline, and the satisfaction of making abstract ideas concrete.

me@nextplane

Travel represents motion, possibility, and perspective. I love airports, city walks, beautiful architecture, waterfront views, and the feeling of stepping into a place that changes the way I see the world. Travel reminds me that life is bigger than routine. It gives me visual memory, atmosphere, and stories I carry with me. Some of my favorite moments come from simply noticing how a place feels — the light, the movement, the sounds, and the details that stay in my head long after I leave.

me@artstudio

I am also deeply drawn to spaces that feel aesthetic, colorful, and intentional. I love red tones, contrast, strong visuals, and environments that feel immersive. This part of me is not separate from the rest — it shapes the way I design, dress, photograph, and build things. It is the side of me that notices the visual language of a place and cares about mood, presentation, and feeling. It also shows up in how I wanted this website to look: bold, personal, and alive.

things that inspire me

  • live music and performance spaces
  • coding projects with visual personality
  • travel memories and city energy
  • art studios, galleries, and creative rooms
  • brain science and human behavior
  • bold websites with immersive design

my favorite kinds of moments

  1. a performance that feels electric
  2. a city view that stops me mid-walk
  3. a project finally working after hours of debugging
  4. a room filled with art, color, and atmosphere
  5. a playlist matching exactly how life feels
  6. discovering a new place that becomes a memory
